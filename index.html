Disease-Free Guarantee

To the best of the licensee’s knowledge, the plants sold by Fireweed Cannabis of Vermont do not harbor known cannabis diseases or pests.

All vegetative plants are propagated from inspected mother stock under documented integrated pest management and intake-quarantine procedures. Every saleable lot is sampled for pesticide residue in accordance with Vermont Cannabis Control Board guidance, and results are published on this page by lot number.
